Management Project - Assignment Structure

Just some pointers for the Management Project assignment thats due on 16 March 2018.

What you are looking to do is take an approach that is informed by your reading. So, if your reading leads you toward an effectual approach and you then seek concepts / tools that support this approach then this makes sense e.g. LEAN, Business Model Canvas

Section 1

Section 1(a) - Restate the assignment question

Section 1(b) - Short cover story - ambition/vision (your vision/ambition, what you have to offer the world & how will you change it, your goal or where will you be in 2018 etc.)

Section 1(c) - Short Context Analysis (Look at the forces pressing against your business model from the outside world, factors you need to consider are trends, rules & regulations, economic climate, competition, customer needs, uncertainties, and technologies), map them out and clarify which after to you and why)

Section 2

Section 2(a) - What does the literature say about approaches to an entrepreneurial new venture (support with references)

Section 2(b) - Based on the literature tell the reader the approach you will take and why

Section 3

Your Business Plan or Business Model Canvas or combination (Hybrid)
