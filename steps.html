Login steps

Step 1. Login to the University Portal

Ulster University Portal.

Step 2. Select 'Library & ICT' from the menu and 'return'.

Step 3. Select 'USearch' tab from the menu or any other tab.

Step 4. Type in 'uls' to bring up University of Ulster and return.

Step 5. Type in your Student email and Network password.

Step 6. Now you can begin your search for ebooks and more.
