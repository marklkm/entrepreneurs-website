Module 1: Innovation & Creativity

This module explores principles and practices associated with creativity and innovation. It exposes students to frameworks and methodologies for idea generation, problem solving and creative collaboration.

Week 1 - Dr. Karl Thomas - Design Thinking & Creativity in Business

Week 2 - Dr. Karl Thomas - Customer Discovery

Week 3 - Dr. Karl Thomas - Problems & Solutions: Knowing your Customer

Week 4 - Dr. Karl Thomas - Ideas into Action

Week 5 - Dr. Karl Thomas - Reflection & Goal Setting

Week 5 - Gillian Roddie - Mindsets

Week 6 - Dr. Karl Thomas - Creative Collaboration

Week 6 to Week 9 - Meet Group Hosts & Group Host Projects - Dublin & Galway

Module runs from September 28, 2020 to November 24, 2020.
