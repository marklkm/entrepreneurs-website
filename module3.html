This module introduces the process involved in creating, leading and scaling entrepreneurial start-up venture.

On successful completion of this module students should be able to: 
  MLO 1: Develop confidence to overcome psychological and practical barriers to returning to work. This is achieved through sharing experiences, creative storytelling, case study discussion and guest lectures.  
MLO 2: Assess skills and abilities to identify relevant career pathways through practical Career Pathway workshops.  
MLO 3: A pply creative problem‐solving skills to challenges and opportunities relating to real industry project. Student will deliver a written report and oral presentation, which will illustrate how they have applied key skills to the project.  
MLO 4: To communicate with and to interact with multiple stakeholders and establish professional networks. Project hosts will provide written feedback to students based on module deliverables (report, presentation).
