Springboard+ TCD Creative Thinking, Innovation & Entrepreneurship

The Postgraduate Certificate in Creative Thinking, Innovation & Entrepreneurship (funded via Springboard) programme offers participants the opportunity to acquire a range of problem solving, creative and lateral thinking skills in an experiential learning environment. Students will engage with real entrepreneurs, business leaders and industry mentors in a dynamic yet informal learning environment. Students simulate the 21st century workplace environment by working on true-life innovation Industry projects where students design creative solutions for factual challenges faced by host organisations.
