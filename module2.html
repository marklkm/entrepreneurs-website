Module 2: Enterprise Development

This module introduces the process involved in creating, leading and scaling entrepreneurial start-up venture in the creative and cultural sectors.

Dr. Karl Thomas - Business Models & Value Proposition Canvas, and Lean Business Model Canvas.

Matt Conaghan - Market Strategy & Customer Segments, and Market Analysis & Research.

Dr. Graham - Budgeting & Financial Planning, and Sources of Capital/Revenue Model.

Dr. Karl Thomas - Creative Entrepreneurialism, and Creative Leadership.

Conor Carmody - IP Management, and Startup Governance.

Module runs from December 7, 2020 to February 9, 2021.
